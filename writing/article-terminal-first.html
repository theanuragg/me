Why Terminals Never Crash: The Case for TUI-First Trading Platforms

Published: August 15, 2025

Terminals are more than just a place to run shell commands. They are high-performance environments for building lightweight, fast, and reliable applications. In this article, I explore the benefits of TUI-first trading platforms and why they are the preferred choice for professional traders.

Benefits of TUI

  • Performance: Terminals use fewer resources than GUIs, allowing for more CPU and memory to be used for critical trading logic.
  • Reliability: Terminals are less prone to crashes and network jitter, making them the perfect environment for high-stakes trading.
  • Efficiency: TUIs allow for more information to be displayed in a single view, allowing traders to make faster and more informed decisions.

Conclusion

TUI-first platforms are the future of professional trading. By building for performance and reliability from day one, we can create tools that stay fast and accurate even under extreme load.
